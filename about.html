About Us

Kier Vergel de Dios is a Filipino nurse,
self-taught artist, and is the founder
of Hinuha Creative.


For seven years, he has worked as a healthcare professional, and now, he ventures into the world of Visual and Graphic Arts, making art projects for friends, family, and acquaintances. With no formal art training, he explored his art potentials through self-help guides.

Today, he continuously hones his craft in watercolor portraits, digital cartoon, and corporate media art. He is currently studying at the Dublin Institute of Design taking up HNC in UX Design and Front-End Development.

Designed, illustrated, and developed by Kier Vergel de Dios.
Created using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
In fulfillment of the term requirement for HNC in UX-Design and Front-End Development,
Dublin Institute of Design, Dublin Ireland.
